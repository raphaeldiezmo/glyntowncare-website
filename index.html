Locate us

Bus Route Option

  • Look for the nearest bus stop to Glyntown Glanmire that is serviced by the bus 214. you can use a map or a transportation app to locate the nearest stop. You can google bus 214 or install moovitapp

    bus 214 schedule
    https://bustimes.org/services/214-cuh-a-and-e-cork-city-patrick-street

    You can also use the movitapp, by clicking the link below
    https://moovitapp.com/index/en/public_transit-line-214-Ireland-502-851897-228786-0

  • Board the bus 214 at the bus stop and make sure it is headed to hazelwood not Knockraha. always ask the driver if you're going to hazelwood instead of Knockraha.
  • Stay on the bus until you reach the last stop.
  • Open your google maps, or the easiest way is to just open the website glyntowncarecentre.ie. Scroll down and click the direction from our website's locate us through the end of the home page.
