About Us

A beacon of exceptional care, providing unwavering support and medical expertise with compassion, respect, and integrity, fostering a warm environment where residents thrive within a community that feels like family.

A Place of Trust and Transparency

we value open communication, working collaboratively with families to understand residents' needs, and ensuring a dedicated team is always available to enhance the quality of life for those in our care.

Our Mission

At Glyntown Care Centre, our mission is to provide compassionate and personalized care to our residents, in a welcoming and supportive environment. We strive to enhance their quality of life by offering a wide range of activities, programs, and services that promote physical, emotional, and social well-being. Our team of skilled and dedicated professionals is committed to delivering the highest standards of care, while respecting each resident's dignity, privacy, and autonomy.

We believe in fostering strong relationships with our residents, their families, and the community, and in creating a home-like atmosphere where everyone feels valued, respected, and supported. We are committed to continuously improving our services and facilities, and to providing a safe and comfortable home for our residents.

Our Commitment to Holistic Well-being

We believe in holistic well-being, addressing physical, emotional, social, and spiritual needs. Our approach involves tailored healthcare, diverse activities, and programs for a purposeful and joyful living experience.

Our Vision

Our vision at Glyntown Care Centre is to be the leading provider of exceptional and innovative care services for seniors in our community. We aim to create a warm and inviting atmosphere where our residents feel at home, supported, and empowered to live life to the fullest. We envision a future where seniors receive the highest quality of care, delivered with compassion, respect, and professionalism.

We are committed to fostering a culture of excellence, innovation, and continuous learning, and to building strong relationships with our residents, their families, and the community. Our goal is to be recognized as a centre of excellence in senior care, where residents thrive, families trust, and employees are proud to work. We are dedicated to creating a better future for seniors, one resident at a time.

Services

24/7 Nursing Care & Medical Cover

Our elderly residents receive round-the-clock nursing care, comprehensive medical coverage, and access to our dedicated GP as well as our multidisciplinary services - Speech and language therapy, dietician, occupational therapy, podiatrist optician, dentistry and community x-ray to ensure their health and well-being are prioritized in our nursing home

Structured Care for Daily Living

We provide personalised daily routines for elderly residents in our nursing home, ensuring they receive the care and attention they need to maintain their physical and emotional well-being

Engaging Activities for Resident Well-Being

We provide a wide array of enjoyable activities such as baking, dancing, singing, crafting, arts, gardening, outings, garden parties, watching films, and listening to music for the elderly residents in our nursing home, promoting their well-being and happiness

Empowering Self-Care

We provide support for our residents' self-care by offering hairdressing, hand massage, physiotherapy, and chiropody services on a quarterly basis, recognizing the importance of self-care in enhancing well-being during the golden years of life in our nursing home

Convenient Laundry Care

Our nursing home provides convenient and reliable laundry services to our residents, ensuring their comfort and cleanliness with the exception of dry cleaning.

Spiritual Nourishment

Our nursing home provides regular mass services every month, complete with visiting priests and the opportunity for Holy Communion and receiving the Sacrament of the sick, allowing our residents to engage in meaningful spiritual practices
