Seven years of compassion, care and unwavering dedication

Our Mission

To provide exceptional care and support to elderly individuals in a safe and comfortable environment. We strive to deliver personalised care plans, respecting the unique needs, preferences, and lifestyles of our residents. Our team of dedicated HCAs and nurses work tirelessly to maintain the highest standards of quality care, promoting independence, dignity, and compassion.

We are committed to creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere where our residents can thrive, while upholding our reputation as a leading nursing home in providing outstanding care to our residents.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be trusted and respected nursing home that exceeds expectations in providing exceptional care to our residents. We strive to create a nurturing and inclusive environment where our residents feel valued, safe, and at home. Through our team of compasionate HCAs, nurses, state-of-the-art faciltiies, and innovative care approaches, we aim to enhance the lives of our residents, promote their well-being, and be recognised as a leader in the field of elderly care.

We envision a future where Glyntown Care Centre is synonymous with excellence, compassion, and commitment to enhancing the quality of life for our residents. f

Services

24 Hour Nursing Care and Medical Cover

Our elderly residents receive round-the-clock nursing care, comprehensive medical coverage, and access to our dedicated GP to ensure their health and well-being are prioritized in our nursing home

Structured Care for Daily Living

We provide personalised daily routines for elderly residents in our nursing home, ensuring they receive the care and attention they need to maintain their physical and emotional well-being

Activities for Our Residents' Enjoyment and Well-Being

We provide a wide array of enjoyable activities such as baking, dancing, singing, crafting, arts, gardening, outings, garden parties, watching films, and listening to music for the elderly residents in our nursing home, promoting their well-being and happiness

Empowering Self-Care

We provide support for our residents' self-care by offering hairdressing, hand massage, physiotherapy, and chiropody services on a weekly basis, recognizing the importance of self-care in enhancing well-being during the golden years of life in our nursing home

Convenient Laundry Care

Our nursing home provides convenient and reliable laundry services to our residents, ensuring their comfort and cleanliness with the exception of dry cleaning.

Spiritual Nourishment

Our nursing home provides regular mass services every week, complete with visiting priests and the opportunity for Holy Communion, allowing our residents to engage in meaningful spiritual practices
