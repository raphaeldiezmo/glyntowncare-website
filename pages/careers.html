Work with Us

Join Glyntown Care team and make a positive impact on the lives of our residents while working in a supportive and compassionate environment.

Working at Glyntown Care Centre

At Glyntown Care Centre, we believe that our team members are the heart and soul of our organization. As a leading care provider in our community, we are committed to delivering the highest quality of care to our residents while fostering a positive and supportive work environment for our staff. Joining the Glyntown Care Centre team means becoming part of a dedicated family that shares a common goal – providing compassionate and exceptional care to those who need it most.

Our Core Values

Our work culture is rooted in a set of core values that guide us in everything we do:

Compassion: We treat every resident and colleague with the same kindness and respect that we would expect for our own loved ones.

Excellence: We are committed to delivering the best possible care by continuously striving for excellence in all aspects of our work.

Teamwork: Collaboration is at the heart of our success. We recognize that every role within our organization is essential, and we work together seamlessly to achieve our mission.

Professionalism: We maintain the highest standards of professionalism and integrity, ensuring that our actions align with our commitment to quality care.

Growth: We provide opportunities for personal and professional growth, supporting our team members in their pursuit of continuous learning and development.

Why Choose a Career with Us

Meaningful Work: Every day, you'll make a difference in the lives of our residents and their families. Your contributions will directly impact their well-being and quality of life. Supportive Environment: We foster a collaborative and inclusive work environment where open communication, mutual respect, and teamwork are valued. Training and Development: We are dedicated to helping you grow in your career. We offer ongoing training and development opportunities to enhance your skills and knowledge. Career Paths: Whether you're just starting out or looking to advance your career, Glyntown Care Centre offers various career paths within caregiving, nursing, administration, and more.
