Job Title: Nurse

Job Summary:

Our nursing home is seeking a compassionate and skilled nurse to join our team. As a nurse, you will be responsible for providing high-quality care to our residents, managing medications, and collaborating with other healthcare professionals to ensure the best possible outcomes for our residents. This is a full-time position, and the successful candidate will work closely with our team to deliver exceptional care to our residents.

Duties and Responsibilities:
  • Provide direct patient care to residents in accordance with their individual care plans.
  • Administer medications and treatments as prescribed by physicians and healthcare professionals.
  • Collaborate with other healthcare professionals to coordinate and manage care for our residents.
  • Monitor and document resident health status, including vital signs and other relevant assessments.
  • Participate in the development and implementation of care plans.
  • Communicate effectively with residents, families, and other healthcare professionals to ensure continuity of care.
  • Assist with the admissions and discharges of residents.
  • Ensure compliance with all federal, state, and local regulations and laws governing healthcare delivery.
Qualifications:
  • Valid nursing license in the state of employment.
  • Minimum of two years of nursing experience, preferably in a long-term care or skilled nursing facility.
  • Strong clinical skills and knowledge of nursing practices and principles.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to work effectively in a team environment.
  • Flexibility to work various shifts, including weekends and holidays.
  • Ability to prioritize and manage multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment.
Benefits:
  • Competitive salary based on experience and qualifications.
  • Paid time off and holidays.
  • Professional development and training opportunities.

If you are a compassionate and skilled nurse looking for a fulfilling career in a supportive environment, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity in our nursing home.

Job Title: Kitchen Porter

Job Summary:

Our nursing home is seeking a dedicated and hardworking kitchen porter to join our culinary team. As a kitchen porter, you will be responsible for maintaining a clean and organized kitchen environment, supporting our chefs and kitchen assistants, and ensuring timely meal service to our residents. This is a full-time position, and the successful candidate will work closely with our team to provide exceptional service to our residents.

Duties and Responsibilities:
  • Clean and sanitize kitchen equipment, utensils, and surfaces.
  • Assist with the organization and inventory of kitchen supplies and food items.
  • Assist with food preparation, including washing, peeling, and chopping ingredients.
  • Collect and dispose of kitchen waste and garbage.
  • Set up and break down serving stations.
  • Assist with the loading and unloading of deliveries.
  • Follow all safety and sanitation procedures and regulations.
  • Perform other duties as assigned by the chef or kitchen manager.
Qualifications:
  • High school diploma or equivalent.
  • Previous experience in a commercial kitchen preferred.
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and stand for extended periods of time.
  • Knowledge of basic food safety and sanitation practices.
  • Flexibility to work various shifts, including weekends and holidays.
Benefits:
  • Paid time off and holidays.
  • Professional development and training opportunities.

If you are a dedicated and hardworking individual with a passion for cleanliness and organization, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity as a kitchen porter in our nursing home.

Job Title: Housekeeper

Job Summary:

Our nursing home is seeking a skilled and detail-oriented housekeeper to join our team. As a housekeeper, you will be responsible for ensuring that our residents' living spaces are clean, comfortable, and safe. This is a full-time position, and the successful candidate will work closely with our team to provide exceptional service to our residents.

Duties and Responsibilities:
  • Clean and sanitize resident rooms, bathrooms, common areas, and other living spaces.
  • Vacuum, sweep, and mop floors.
  • Dust and polish furniture, fixtures, and surfaces.
  • Change and launder linens and towels.
  • Stock and organize cleaning supplies and amenities.
  • Follow all safety and sanitation procedures and regulations.
  • Report any maintenance issues or concerns to the appropriate staff.
  • Assist with other housekeeping tasks as needed.
Qualifications:
  • High school diploma or equivalent.
  • Previous housekeeping experience in a healthcare or hospitality setting preferred.
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Ability to lift up to 25 pounds and stand for extended periods of time.
  • Knowledge of basic cleaning and sanitation practices.
  • Flexibility to work various shifts, including weekends and holidays.
Salary and Benefits:
  • Paid time off and holidays.
  • Professional development and training opportunities.

If you are a detail-oriented and dedicated individual with a passion for providing a clean and comfortable living environment for others, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity as a housekeeper in our nursing home.

Job Title: Health Care Assistant

Job Summary:

Our nursing home is seeking a compassionate and skilled health care assistant to join our team. As a health care assistant, you will be responsible for providing assistance and support to our residents with daily living activities, while promoting their physical and emotional well-being. This is a full-time position, and the successful candidate will work closely with our team to provide exceptional care to our residents.

Duties and Responsibilities:
  • Assist residents with personal hygiene tasks, such as bathing, grooming, and toileting.
  • Assist with mobility and transferring residents to and from beds, chairs, and other areas.
  • Help residents with eating, drinking, and other activities of daily living.
  • Monitor residents' physical and emotional well-being, and report any changes or concerns to nursing staff.
  • Provide companionship and emotional support to residents.
  • Participate in resident care planning and goal setting.
  • Follow all safety and infection control procedures and regulations.
  • Document resident care activities and observations accurately and promptly.
Qualifications:
  • Candidate must be studying towards a nursing career or have an appropriate FETAC level 5 or QQI qualification
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Compassionate and patient demeanor.
  • Knowledge of basic health care procedures and infection control practices.
  • Flexibility to work various shifts, including weekends and holidays.
Benefits:
  • Paid time off and holidays.
  • Professional development and training opportunities.

If you are a compassionate and skilled individual with a passion for providing quality care to others, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity as a health care assistant in our nursing home.
