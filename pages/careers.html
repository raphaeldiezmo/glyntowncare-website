Work with Us

Join Glyntown Care team and make a positive impact on the lives of our residents while working in a supportive and compassionate environment.

Health Care Assistant

We are looking for a compassionate Health Care Assistant to join our nursing home team. The successful candidate will be responsible for providing direct care to our residents, including assisting with personal hygiene, administering medications, and monitoring vital signs. The ideal candidate should have excellent communication and interpersonal skills, as well as a strong work ethic and a commitment to providing exceptional patient care. Experience in a similar role is preferred, but not required. The role requires empathy and a kind demeanor towards the elderly residents. Certification as a Health Care Assistant or equivalent is preferred.

Nurse

We are seeking a qualified and compassionate Nurse to join our nursing home team. The ideal candidate must have a nursing degree and be responsible for providing direct patient care, administering medications , and working collaboratively with our healthcare team to ensure the well-being of our residents. This position requires excellent communication , critical thinking , and problem-solving skills , as well as a commitment to providing exceptional patient care. Experience in geriatric nursing is preferred.

Kitchen Assistant

We are looking for a span.highlighted-detail reliable and hardworking Kitchen Assistant to join our nursing home team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for maintaining a clean and organized kitchen, washing dishes and utensils, and assisting with food preparation as needed. The role requires physical stamina and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment. The successful candidate should have excellent time management and multitasking skills, as well as a commitment to maintaining a safe and hygienic kitchen environment. Experience in a similar role is preferred, but not required.

Household Staff

We are seeking a dedicated and detail-oriented Household Staff to join our nursing home team. The successful candidate will be responsible for maintaining a clean, safe, and comfortable living environment for our residents. This includes cleaning resident rooms and common areas, doing laundry, and assisting with meal service as needed. The ideal candidate should have excellent organizational skills, the ability to prioritize tasks effectively, and a strong work ethic. Experience in a similar role is preferred, but not required. The role requires empathy and a kind demeanor towards the elderly residents.
